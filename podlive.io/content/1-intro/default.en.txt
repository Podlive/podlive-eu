Title: Intro

----

Text: 

With Podlive you can listen to live streaming Podcasts. We currently support all podcast livestreams broadcasting via (link: #ultraschall text: Ultraschall with Studio Link OnAir). You can mark your favourite channels to get notified via push notifications immediately when a channel starts streaming.

Podlive is a collaborative project of [Stefan Trauth](https://stefantrauth.de) (iOS), [Frank Gregor](https://cocoanaut.com) (macOS) and [Sven Hennessen](https://hennessen.net) (Android).
